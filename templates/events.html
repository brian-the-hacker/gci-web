About the Event

This event celebrates the anniversary of Gospel Centers International Machakos, bringing together believers and leaders to reflect on the church’s impact and to discuss the future of ministry, discipleship, and community outreach. Hosted by Gospel Centers International, this gathering will highlight God’s faithfulness and explore how we can continue to fulfill the Great Commission with excellence. Join us for a time of fellowship, worship, and inspiration as we look forward to the future and strengthen our commitment to advancing the Gospel.
